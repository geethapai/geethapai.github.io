Marketing & Services Website

Information Architecture & Wireframes

top

Problem

A unique sports-related marketing agency needed a website redesign to showcase their services and amazing value-add. The company plays an integral role in some of the largest, most visibile sports events in the world, but this is not evident from looking at their site. Their current site is dated and while they provide a variety of services it’s difficult to tell what their central role and value-add is for clients.

Role

My responsibilities were to develop an information architecture map of the company's current website, work with other designers to devise the new, simplified information architecture map based on user goals and design high-level conceptual wireframes based on initial stakeholder meetings.

Process

  • 2 half day meetings with company stakeholders to understand companies mission and goals for redesign
  • Empathy mapping to understand all the potential users' pain points and goals
  • Mapped existing IA
  • Developed new IA based on both business goals and main user goals
  • Designed conceptual wireframes based on user goals to help clarify the company's value add

Result

The client approved our high-level concepts and the project is still ongoing.
