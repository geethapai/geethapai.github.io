Mobile Banking Application

Strategy, Information Architecture & UI Design

top

Problem

A major regional bank asked Fresh Tilled Soil for its UX thinking, strategy and UI design with their new mobile banking app. This project was unique in that the client was working with outside developers who had created initial screens for part of the app. However, the client asked us to step in and redo the overall user experience, information architecture (IA), as well as the user interface. I worked with several designers on various components of this project, including the app’s information architecture, user flows and user interface.

Role

My responsiblities were to work with colleagues to redesign the information architecture for the app based on user goals, rethink the information architecture for the Bill Pay portion of the app. In addition, I designed the user flows for the Accounts section and developed high fidelity wireframes for the Accounts section.

Process

  • Clarify user goals through conversations with clients
  • Sketching to think through app's information architecture, as well as user flows and user interface designs for the app's Accounts section
  • Design high fidelity wireframes for the Accounts section in Photoshop

Result

The IA mapping and initial UI development have served as good discussion points to validate our thinking around the user experience, information architecture and visual design. While the project continues to be ongoing, the client has approved our UX strategy thus far. The app will be released in 2015.
