Product Verticals

Cutting-Edge IoT: Enterprise Companies and Startups

While at Favorite Medium, I’ve had the opportunity to solve design challenges related to cutting-edge technology, such as:

  • edge devices in physical spaces synced to mobile devices
  • sensors worn on the body for disease-condition management
  • biometrics verifications for a security app
  • utility company-level solar battery management and power deployment.

Startup MVP Roadmapping, Research and Design

I’ve worked on multiple products for a multitude of early stage startups. Through those projects, I’ve conducted user and stakeholder research, assisted startup founders in prioritizing features for their MVP roadmaps, created designs for concept testing to validate design decisions, designed journey maps, sitemaps and wireframes, created user stories and worked with project managers and developers to execute and build MVP products.

Startup products include:

  • portfolio company platform management tool for a global cleantech startup incubator
  • auto-repair educational platform
  • sommelier training iPad app/prototype
  • event security biometrics mobile app and desktop tool.

Community Impact and Social Justice

Over the last few years, I’ve worked on multiple social good-related projects:

  • VR app for an animal rights organization depicting the hidden side of factory farming
  • research, design and prototyping a new concept for a city services website
  • design workshops for Oakland Digital’s design platform to connect up-and-coming designers with local businesses.
  • Oakland Digital's design-thinking workshop at Google.org to gather research for #OaklandCommunityPlace, a new Oakland Digital space
