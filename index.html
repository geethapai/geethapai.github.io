Thanks for visiting my website! I’m a UX designer with a background in social science and psychology. What drew me to UX design is my fascination with human behavior and a desire to create experiences that resonate with people and help them accomplish their goals. I’ve worked for and been trained by one of the leading UX design firms in the Northeast – Fresh Tilled Soil. My skills set is UX strategy, wireframing, sketching and prototyping. Please have a look at my portfolio to learn about the projects I have worked on, as well as my github page. If you’d like to talk more, please get in touch!
