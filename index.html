Howdy and welcome to my site! I’m a UX designer at Favorite Medium, a product design firm in the SF Bay Area. I work on a range of mobile and web applications, including IoT (internet of things) products that leverage sensors and other technology. My skill set includes user research, UX design and strategy.

My background is in psychology and education. I’m fascinated by human behavior and what makes people tick! I really enjoy talking to people, learning about their needs and designing products to help them accomplish a goal or a task.

Feel free to check out my github page or the rest of my site, which I designed and coded. And if you’d like to say “hi”, check out the social media links at the bottom of this page or shoot me an email.
